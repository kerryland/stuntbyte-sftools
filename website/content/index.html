--- title: Welcome to Stuntbyte! ---

We provide tools to help Salesforce Developers work more productively with the salesforce.com and database.com platforms.

  • Stuntbyte JDBC - Our JDBC driver for Salesforce. It makes querying and updating Salesforce simple, and allows the use of best-of-breed database query tools. It also helps with data migration and reporting.
  • Stuntbyte Deploy - Our deployment tool for Salesforce. It takes away much of the pain of Salesforce deployments.
  • Stuntbyte IDE - Our replacement for the "Force IDE". It lets developers use quality editors like Sublime Text 2 and VIM to compile, test, and navigate Salesforce Apex code.
